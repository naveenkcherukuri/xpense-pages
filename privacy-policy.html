Introduction

Xpense ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. IMPORTANT: Xpense is a 100% LOCAL app. ALL your data is stored ONLY on your device. We do NOT have any servers storing your data. Optional backups are stored in YOUR OWN Google Drive account, never on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information automatically collected when you use our app.

1.1 Personal Information

  • Google Sign-in: Used ONLY to access YOUR Google Drive for optional backups (we do NOT store your email, name, or any Google account data on our servers)
  • Biometric Data: Face Recognition (Face ID) or Fingerprint data stored locally on your device for app security (never leaves your device)

1.2 Financial Data (Stored ONLY on Your Device)

  • Transaction Information: Amounts, dates, categories, notes, descriptions, attachments (photos/receipts)
  • Account Information: Account names, types, balances, bank names, card networks, account numbers (encrypted locally)
  • Budget Information: Budget limits, categories, periods (monthly/quarterly/yearly), alert thresholds (50-95%)
  • Category & Tag System: Custom categories, tags, icons, and color coding for organization
  • Vendor Information: Merchant names, contact details, transaction history
  • Recurring Transactions: Recurring rules, payment schedules, reminder settings (daily/weekly/monthly/yearly)
  • Custom Fields: User-defined transaction fields with validation rules

1.3 Usage Data (Optional Anonymous Analytics)

  • App Usage: Features used, screens visited, interaction patterns (NO financial data)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, platform (iOS/Android)
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics via Firebase Analytics (ONLY with your explicit consent)
  • Performance Monitoring: App performance metrics, loading times (NO personal data)
  • Error Logs: Application errors for debugging (NO financial or personal data included)

1.4 Media and Files (Stored Locally)

  • Receipt Photos: Images captured or selected for transaction documentation (stored locally)
  • Document Attachments: PDFs, images attached to transactions (stored on your device)
  • Camera Access: Used only when you take photos for receipts (NOT accessed without permission)
  • Storage Access: Used only for importing/exporting transaction data (CSV, Excel, PDF formats)
  • Export Files: Transaction reports, budget summaries you generate for backup

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes (ALL PROCESSING HAPPENS LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE):

  • Core Financial Management: Track expenses, income, budgets, accounts, categories, vendors, tags - all processed locally on your device
  • Smart Budgeting: Calculate budget progress, spending trends, category breakdowns with alerts at 50-95% thresholds (local processing)
  • Transaction Management: Store and organize transactions with custom fields, tags, attachments, recurring rules (local database)
  • Local AI Assistant (XpenseAI): Provide financial insights using Gemma AI models running entirely on your device - your data NEVER leaves your device
  • Reports & Analytics: Generate monthly/yearly spending reports, category breakdowns, vendor analysis (all calculated locally)
  • Smart Notifications: Send budget alerts, bill reminders, recurring transaction notifications based on your data (processed locally)
  • Biometric Security: Protect app access with Face ID/Touch ID (biometric data stored only in device secure enclave, never transmitted)
  • Optional Google Drive Backup: Encrypt and backup your data to YOUR OWN Google Drive if you enable this feature
  • Anonymous App Analytics: Only if you consent, basic feature usage statistics (NO financial data) sent to Firebase Analytics
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries via email (we have NO access to your app data)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • LOCAL STORAGE ONLY: All financial data (transactions, accounts, budgets, categories, tags, vendors, custom fields) stored in encrypted Drift/SQLite database on YOUR device
  • NO SERVERS COLLECTING DATA: We have ZERO servers storing your personal or financial information
  • Database Encryption: Drift database with encryption at rest, secure storage in app sandbox
  • Optional Google Drive Backup: If enabled, encrypted backup stored in YOUR OWN Google Drive account (you control it, not us)
  • Biometric Security: Face ID/Touch ID data NEVER leaves device secure enclave - handled entirely by iOS/Android OS
  • Secure Preferences: App settings stored in encrypted SharedPreferences and Flutter Secure Storage
  • Access Controls: App lock, biometric authentication, privacy mode to hide sensitive information
  • On-Device AI (XpenseAI): Gemma AI models run entirely on your device - NO external AI services, NO cloud processing, NO data transmission
  • Attachment Security: Receipt photos and documents stored securely in local app storage
  • Session Management: Authentication tokens stored locally - no server-side session tracking
  • Complete Privacy: Even we cannot access your data - it exists only on your device or YOUR Google Drive

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Services

  • Payment Processing: We do NOT process or store payment information - all handled by App Store/Play Store
  • Subscription Status: Only subscription status (active/inactive) stored locally to enable premium features

4.3 AI Services (100% On-Device)

  • Gemma AI Models: Downloaded from Firebase Storage and run entirely on your device for financial insights
  • XpenseAI Features: Natural language queries, spending insights, budget recommendations - all processed locally
  • Voice Recording: If you use voice features, audio processed locally on device - NEVER sent to external servers
  • No External AI Services: We do NOT use OpenAI, ChatGPT, Claude, or any cloud-based AI services
  • Complete Privacy: Your financial conversations with AI stay on your device
